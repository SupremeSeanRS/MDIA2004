Preparation

In order to prepare for my journey to school I will firstly have to be awake. My preparations then move on to packing of my bag to ensure that I have all the materials I need at school. After that I go to shower, brush my teeth and anything hygienic related. Immediately after, I drink water to ensure that I have water in my systems from early for better health values and then I eat breakfast. I also prepare the vehicle by checking that everything is okay and that there is gas as a way to ensure that I will be able to make it to school safely.
