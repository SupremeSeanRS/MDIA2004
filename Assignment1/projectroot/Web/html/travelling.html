Travelling

After leaving home and travelling to school. I would have to pass through Lucea which is the capital of the parish Hanover and is approximately 10 minutes away from where I live. After passing through Lucea, the next major town I would have to pass through is Sandy Bay and then Hopewell. Hopewell is approximately 20-25 minutes out of Lucea and in my journey to school, it would have been 35 minutes already that have passed from when I left home to Hopewell. After Hopewell, I would have to pass through the State of Emergency Checkpoint at Great River which borders the parishes of Hanover and St. James. After entering St. James, I have to pass Bogue then go on the Howard Cooke Blvd then to Queens Drive or Jimmy Cliff Blvd to come to school.
